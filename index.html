How It Works

Four modules. One platform. Less admin.

1

Churn Alerts

StudioPulse tracks check-in frequency, booking patterns, and visit recency for every member. When someone starts drifting — fewer visits, missed classes, longer gaps — you get an alert 2 weeks before they're likely to cancel. Reach out while there's still time to win them back.

2

Automated Sub Coverage

When a class loses an instructor or members cancel, StudioPulse automatically notifies your waitlist, promotes the next person in line, and confirms the update — no texts, no calls, no scramble.

3

Billing Recovery

Failed card? StudioPulse retries at optimal intervals, sends a friendly recovery email, and notifies the member to update their card. Most recoveries happen within 48 hours. You focus on coaching — we handle collections.

4

Class Scheduling

One calendar. All your instructors, all your classes, all your bookings. Members book online; you see it in real time.
